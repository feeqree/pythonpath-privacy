Privacy Policy for PythonPath

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

1. Developer Information

  • App Name: PythonPath
  • Developer Name: ARIF FIKRI BIN AMIR
  • Package Name: com.pythonpath.learning.game
  • Contact Email: af03.af30@gmail.com

2. Introduction

PythonPath ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Learning Progress Data

  • Quiz results and scores
  • Topic mastery levels
  • Learning streaks
  • Points and achievements
  • App usage statistics

3.2 Device Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • App version
  • Usage analytics (for app improvement)

3.3 No Personal Identifiable Information

We do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address (unless you contact support)
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Photos or files

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track your progress and provide insights
  • Improve app features and content
  • Analyze app performance
  • Provide customer support

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Used for generating adaptive quiz questions
  • Data sent: Topic name, difficulty level, user progress summary
  • No personal information is shared

5.2 Huawei Mobile Services (HMS)

  • Analytics Kit: Tracks app usage for improvement
  • Push Kit: Sends learning reminders (optional)
  • Account Kit: User authentication (if enabled)

5.3 Local Storage

  • All learning progress is stored locally on your device
  • Data is not transmitted to external servers (except analytics)

6. Data Security

  • All data is stored locally using secure storage
  • We use industry-standard encryption
  • No sensitive data is transmitted over unsecured connections

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View all stored learning progress
  • Delete your data: Clear all app data from device settings
  • Export your data: Request a copy of your progress (contact support)
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics in app settings

8. Children's Privacy

PythonPath is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

9. Data Retention

  • Learning progress is stored locally on your device
  • Data is retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Analytics data is anonymized and retained for up to 2 years

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights:

  • Email: af03.af30@gmail.com
  • In-App: Go to Profile → Settings → Contact Support
